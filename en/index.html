Agency

Founded in 2016 and based in Montpellier, IKOM is an international print and web communication agency specializing in tourism and travel agency support. Our objective ? Let you benefit from our expertise in Graphics and the Web to develop and reach a new target. Consisting of a young and dynamic team, we make every effort to realize your projects.

In order to fulfill your craziest desires you will be supported by two designers of websites specializing in design and ergonomics. But IKOM is also two graphic designers passionate and professional, strong proposals that will transcribe the character of your company.

Composed of a team with multiplex talents, the agency IKOM accompanies you in your search for notoriety. Throughout your project we remain at your disposal and do everything possible to achieve your objectives.

Whether you are in France or around the world, you can benefit from our expertise in the field of Communication. Indeed, our agency is present in many countries given that our team is multilingual. It is then also possible to have an international scope.
